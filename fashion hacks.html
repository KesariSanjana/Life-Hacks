1.Zip Hack

VIDEOS



1.When Your Zipper Seems Stiff…making a bag zipper run smooth with candle wax
2.When your zipper seems stiff and neither goes up nor down
> here’s what to do: run a candle or crayon wax, Vaseline or the end of a graphite pencil along the teeth of your zipper.
This way, it loosens up and slides up and down smoothly.


2.Stain Removal




1.Use Baby Powder to Get Rid of Oil Stains
2.Removing oil stains with baby powder
3.Once you notice an oil stain on your trousers, don’t fret or worry about getting rid of it.
4.Simply sprinkle baby powder on the stained area before washing it off.


3. Clear Nail Polish for Loose Sunglasses




1.When you wear your sunglasses and it feels like they no longer fit your face.
2.That means they are now loose.
3. Don’t put them in the trash can yet.
4. Apply a clear nail polish around the hinges to tighten it.


4.Buttons strengthening using nail polish




1.Use Clear Nail Polish to Keep Your Buttons in Place,strengthening shirt button with nail polish
2.When you notice a button on your shirt is surviving on a thin thread.
3.If you don’t want to stitch it, apply clear nail polish on the threads.
4. If there’s no nail polish, white glue will also help to prevent the threads from getting frayed.
